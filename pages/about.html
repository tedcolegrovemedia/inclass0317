About Pizza Haven

Pizza Haven started with one goal: serve bold, delicious pizza using simple ingredients and traditional techniques.

Every day we prepare fresh dough, simmer our house sauce, and grate our cheese in-house to make every slice memorable.

We are proud to be a local favorite for family dinners, game nights, and late-night cravings.
